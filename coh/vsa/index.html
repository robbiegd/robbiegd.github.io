Explore Humanities Study Abroad Locations

Our faculty and students have captured some exciting points of interest around the world.

On Mobile (with viewer)

Select cardboard mode in the bottom right corner of the screen. Hover the dark circle in your viewer to navigate to the 360 images for that location. You can navigate to other places by hovering on orbs within the image To exit the location, hover on the orb at the bottom of the screen.

On Desktop/Mobile (without viewer)

Best if viewed in full screen. Click the full screen square in the bottom right corner. Click on the orbs to navigate to the 360 images for that location. You can navigate to other places by clicking on orbs within the image To exit the location, click on the orb at the bottom of the screen.
